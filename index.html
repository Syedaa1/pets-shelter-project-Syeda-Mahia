Our Philosophy

At Loved Pets Animal Rescue, we believe all animals deserve a chance to find a loving forever home. We provide initial veterinary care to rescued animals, then work to match each animal with a human family to love them and keep them safe forever. All animals are screened for diseases upon arrival, washed and groomed, and then added to our website for adoptive families to see.

Potential adoptive families are screened for adoption history, household lifestyle, and commitment to providing a safe and loving home.

How to Become a Volunteer

  1. Complete the online application

  3. Participate in a brief phone interview
  4. Attend a volunteer training session on the first Saturday of every month
